Interaction Design is program that focuses on user experience and UI elements with coding. This program also focuses on teaching students how to collect and use data and process it . This program also introduces students with workflow in Illustrator, Photoshop, Maya and html/css. Interaction design creates great introduction in many forms in design such 3D modelling , UI interaces and many more for students to learn and pursue in any direction they want to work.
